This is SeatFrog

Making upgrades effortless

It all started with the simple idea of taking  the pain out of upgrading.

For airlines we started by changing the way they perceive integration, building a robust platform that can be live in days not months. Then we enabled full automation of the upgrade process right up to the gate, instantly re-issuing tickets direct to mobile. Whilst for passengers no more mystery processes or witty one liners. Just a simple, intuitively designed experience that fits seamlessly  with their journey.

Upgrading should be effortless for everyone, but somehow it’s become complicated.  We’re here to change that.
